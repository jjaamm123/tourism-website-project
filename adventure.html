Extreme Sports in Nepal

Nepal isn't just about temples and trekking - it's an adventure playground! From white-water rafting in raging rivers to paragliding over Pokhara's lakes, Nepal offers some of the world's most thrilling adventure sports.

With diverse landscapes ranging from Himalayan peaks to tropical jungles, you'll find adrenaline-pumping activities for every level of adventurer.

All our adventure activities are operated by certified professionals with international safety standards. Your thrill is our priority!

Frequently Asked Questions

The best seasons are Spring (March-May) and Autumn (September-November). However, some activities like paragliding and bungee jumping operate year-round. Monsoon season (June-August) is best for white-water rafting.

Most activities offer options for complete beginners. Activities are graded by difficulty level. We provide training and guidance for first-timers. Only a few extreme sports require previous experience.

Comfortable sportswear, closed shoes, sunscreen, water bottle. Specific gear is provided for each activity. We'll send you a detailed packing list after booking.

Most activities: Minimum 12-16 years, maximum 65-70 years. Weight limits vary (typically 100-120kg). Health restrictions apply for certain activities. Contact us for specific requirements.
