Everest View Trek

Himalayan panoramas without extreme altitude trekking

Tour Overview

Experience the magic of the Everest region without the challenges of extreme altitude. This 7-day trek takes you to Namche Bazaar and Tengboche Monastery with spectacular views of Everest, Lhotse, Nuptse, and Ama Dablam. Perfect for those wanting to experience Sherpa culture and Himalayan beauty.

Trek Details

  • Duration: 7 days
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Max Altitude: 3,860m
  • Flight: Kathmandu-Lukla-Kathmandu
  • Best for: First-time trekkers

Mountain Views

  • Mount Everest (8,848m)
  • Lhotse (8,516m)
  • Nuptse (7,861m)
  • Ama Dablam (6,812m)
  • Thamserku (6,623m)

Inclusions

  • Round-trip Lukla flights
  • Experienced Sherpa guide
  • Porter service
  • Tea house accommodation
  • All meals during trek

Price

From $850 per person
