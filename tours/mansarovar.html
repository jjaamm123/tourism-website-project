Kailash & Lake Manasarovar Pilgrimage

Complete pilgrimage to Tibet's most sacred mountain and lake

Tour Overview

The ultimate Himalayan pilgrimage combining Mount Kailash kora with Lake Manasarovar circumambulation. This 18-day journey is considered one of the most spiritually significant pilgrimages in Asia, offering purification and enlightenment according to multiple religious traditions.

Pilgrimage Elements

  • Mount Kailash kora (52km)
  • Lake Manasarovar parikrama (90km)
  • Holy dip in Manasarovar
  • Rakshas Tal viewing
  • Tirthapuri hot springs

Spiritual Benefits

  • Cleanses all sins of lifetime
  • Brings liberation (moksha)
  • Purification through holy dip
  • Connection with divine energies
  • Life transformation

Inclusions

  • All required Tibet permits
  • Full support team (guide, cook, helpers)
  • Yak/porter support for kora
  • Camping equipment
  • All meals and transportation
