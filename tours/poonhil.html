Ghorepani Poon Hill Trek

Short trek with panoramic Himalayan sunrise views

Tour Overview

The Ghorepani Poon Hill Trek is the perfect introduction to Himalayan trekking. This 5-day moderate trek offers stunning views of Dhaulagiri, Annapurna I, and Machhapuchhre (Fishtail) mountains. Experience Gurung and Magar village culture along the trail.

Trek Details

  • Duration: 5 days
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Max Altitude: 3,210m
  • Best Seasons: Spring/Autumn
  • Accommodation: Tea houses

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive to Nayapul, trek to Tikhedhunga
  • Day 2: Trek to Ghorepani
  • Day 3: Poon Hill sunrise, trek to Tadapani
  • Day 4: Trek to Ghandruk
  • Day 5: Return to Pokhara

Inclusions

  • Experienced trekking guide
  • Porter service (1 porter per 2 clients)
  • All meals during trek
  • Tea house accommodation
  • Permits and transportation

Price

From $720 per person

Based on group of 4
