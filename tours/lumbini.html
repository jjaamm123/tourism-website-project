Lumbini Spiritual Tour

Journey to the birthplace of Lord Buddha

Tour Overview

Visit Lumbini, the birthplace of Siddhartha Gautama (Lord Buddha) and one of the most important spiritual sites in the world. This 2-day tour explores the Sacred Garden, Mayadevi Temple, and international monasteries representing different Buddhist traditions.

Highlights

  • Mayadevi Temple (exact birthplace)
  • Sacred Garden complex
  • International monasteries
  • Ashoka Pillar
  • Peaceful meditation spaces

International Monasteries

  • Chinese Monastery
  • Korean Temple
  • Thai Monastery
  • German Vihara
  • Myanmar Golden Temple

  • Vegetarian meals
