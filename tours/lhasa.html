Lhasa & Central Tibet Tour

Explore the heart of Tibetan Buddhism and culture

Tour Overview

Journey to the "Roof of the World" and discover Tibet's spiritual and cultural heart. This 6-day tour explores Lhasa's most sacred sites including the Potala Palace, Jokhang Temple, and surrounding monasteries. Experience Tibetan Buddhist traditions and stunning Himalayan landscapes.

Main Attractions

  • Potala Palace (UNESCO)
  • Jokhang Temple
  • Barkhor Street
  • Sera & Drepung Monasteries
  • Norbulingka Palace

Tibetan Culture

  • Monk debates at Sera
  • Prayer wheel turning
  • Butter lamp offerings
  • Thangka painting
  • Traditional Tibetan food
