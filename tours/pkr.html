Pokhara Lakes & Mountains Tour

Lakeside relaxation with breathtaking Himalayan views

Tour Overview

Escape to the serene beauty of Pokhara, Nepal's adventure capital. This 4-day tour combines lakeside relaxation with stunning mountain views. Experience the tranquility of Phewa Lake, witness sunrise over the Annapurna range from Sarangkot, and explore natural wonders.

Highlights

  • Sunrise views from Sarangkot
  • Boating on Phewa Lake
  • Visit World Peace Pagoda
  • Explore Gupteshwor Cave
  • David's Fall waterfall

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival, Lakeside walk
  • Day 2: Sarangkot sunrise, boating
  • Day 3: Peace Pagoda, caves
  • Day 4: Free time, departure

Inclusions

  • All transportation
  • Three nights accommodation
  • Expert guide
  • Breakfast daily
  • All entrance fees

From $300 per person

Based on double occupancy
