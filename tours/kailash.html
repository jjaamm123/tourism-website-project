Mount Kailash Pilgrimage

Sacred kora around the most holy mountain in Asia

Tour Overview

Undertake the ultimate spiritual journey to Mount Kailash, considered sacred by Hindus, Buddhists, Jains, and Bonpos. This 15-day pilgrimage includes the challenging but transformative 3-day kora (circumambulation) around the mountain at altitudes over 5,000m.

Pilgrimage Details

  • Duration: 15 days
  • Kora Distance: 52km
  • Max Altitude: 5,630m (Drolma La)
  • Best Time: May-October
  • Difficulty: Challenging

Spiritual Significance

  • Hindu: Abode of Lord Shiva
  • Buddhist: Axis Mundi
  • Jain: Site of first Tirthankara
  • Bon: Sacred nine-story mountain
  • One kora cleanses sins of lifetime

Inclusions

  • All Tibet permits (including Alien's, Military, Kailash)
  • Yak/porter for kora
  • Experienced guide and cook
  • Camping equipment
  • All meals during trek
