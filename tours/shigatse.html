Lhasa – Gyantse – Shigatse Tour

Classic overland route through Tibet's cultural heartland

Tour Overview

Follow the classic Tibet overland route from Lhasa to Shigatse via Gyantse. This 8-day journey explores ancient monasteries, fortresses, and traditional Tibetan towns. Experience the diversity of Tibetan Buddhism across different schools and regions.

Route Highlights

  • Lhasa: Potala Palace, Jokhang
  • Yamdrok Lake (turquoise)
  • Gyantse: Kumbum Stupa
  • Shigatse: Tashilhunpo
  • Karola Glacier views

Cultural Sites

  • Palkhor Monastery (Gyantse)
  • Tashilhunpo Monastery (Shigatse)
  • Gyantse Dzong (fortress)
  • Traditional villages
  • Local markets

Inclusions

  • All Tibet permits
  • Private vehicle with driver
  • Expert Tibetan guide
  • All accommodations
  • Meals as specified
