Chitwan Jungle Safari

Wildlife adventure in Nepal's first national park

Tour Overview

Experience the thrill of wildlife spotting in Chitwan National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. This 3-day safari tour offers elephant safaris, jungle walks, canoe rides, and cultural performances with the indigenous Tharu community.

Wildlife Spotting

  • One-horned rhinoceros
  • Royal Bengal tigers
  • Wild elephants
  • Over 500 bird species
  • Crocodiles & gharials

Activities

  • Elephant back safari
  • Jungle walking tour
  • Canoe ride on Rapti River
  • Visit elephant breeding center
  • Tharu cultural show

Inclusions

  • All safari activities
  • Expert naturalist guide
  • Jungle resort accommodation
  • All meals in Chitwan
  • Transportation

Price

From $290 per person

Based on double occupancy
