Rock Climbing

Scale vertical walls in the heart of the Himalayas

Adventure Overview

Test your strength and technique on Nepal's natural and artificial climbing walls. From beginner-friendly indoor climbing to challenging outdoor routes, we offer climbing experiences for all skill levels in safe, controlled environments.

Our certified climbing instructors provide personalized coaching and ensure all safety protocols are followed. Whether you're trying climbing for the first time or looking to advance your skills, we have the perfect route for you.

Climbing Options

  • Indoor Wall: Half/full day, beginner
  • Nagarkot Crag: Day trip, intermediate
  • Hattiban Rock: Full day, advanced
  • Multi-Pitch: Full day, experienced
  • Bouldering: Half day, all levels

Inclusions

  • Certified climbing guide
  • All climbing equipment
  • Safety harness and helmet
  • Climbing shoes (rental)
  • Instruction and coaching
  • Transportation (outdoor)
  • First aid support

What to Bring

  • Comfortable athletic wear
  • Climbing gloves (optional)
  • Water bottle
  • Energy snacks
  • Sunscreen
  • Small backpack

Pricing

  • Indoor Half Day: $55-75
  • Indoor Full Day: $90-120
  • Outdoor Day Trip: $120-180
  • Multi-Day Course: $250-400

Prices include all equipment and instruction
