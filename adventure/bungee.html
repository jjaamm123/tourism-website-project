Bungee Jumping

Take the 160m plunge into Bhote Koshi Gorge

Adventure Overview

Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush with Nepal's highest bungee jump! Plunge 160 meters from a suspension bridge into the breathtaking Bhote Koshi gorge, one of the most spectacular bungee locations in the world.

Operated by internationally certified jump masters with Swiss-designed equipment, this jump meets the highest safety standards. The bridge is designed specifically for bungee jumping and offers a thrilling free fall experience.

Jump Options

  • Standard Jump: 160m free fall
  • Combo Package: Bungee + Swing
  • Night Jump: Special night experience
  • Group Jump: 4+ people discount
  • Tandem Jump: Jump with a friend

Inclusions

  • High-quality bungee equipment
  • Safety briefing and training
  • Transportation from Kathmandu
  • Certificate of achievement
  • Comprehensive insurance

  • Extra clothes
