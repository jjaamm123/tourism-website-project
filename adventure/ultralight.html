Ultra Light Flight

Fly in an open cockpit over the Himalayas

Adventure Overview

Experience the thrill of flying in an open-cockpit ultralight aircraft over the stunning Pokhara Valley. With panoramic views of the Annapurna range, Machhapuchhre (Fishtail Mountain), and Phewa Lake, this is aviation adventure at its finest.

Our ultralight flights are conducted by experienced pilots with modern, well-maintained aircraft. Feel the wind in your face as you soar like a bird over some of the world's most spectacular scenery.

Flight Options

  • Scenic Flight: 30 minutes, valley views
  • Mountain Flight: 60 minutes, Annapurna views
  • Sunrise/Sunset: 45 minutes, golden hour
  • Extended Tour: 90 minutes, comprehensive
  • Photography Flight: 45 minutes, camera friendly

Inclusions

  • Experienced pilot
  • Safety briefing
  • Flight jacket and gear
  • Headset for communication
  • Flight certificate
  • Transport to airfield
  • Basic insurance

What to Bring

  • Warm layers
  • Sunglasses
  • Camera with strap
  • Comfortable shoes
  • Water bottle
  • Personal ID

Pricing

  • 30-Minute Flight: $120-150
  • 60-Minute Flight: $200-250
  • Sunset Flight: $180-220
  • 90-Minute Tour: $280-350

Prices vary by season and flight route
