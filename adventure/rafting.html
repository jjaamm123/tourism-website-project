White Water Rafting

Conquer the raging rivers of the Himalayas

Adventure Overview

Experience the thrill of white water rafting on Nepal's world-class rivers. From the scenic Trishuli to the challenging Bhote Koshi and the epic multi-day Sun Koshi expedition, we offer rafting trips for all skill levels.

All trips are led by internationally certified river guides with extensive safety training. We provide top-quality rafting equipment and comprehensive safety briefings before every trip.

River Options

  • Trishuli River: 1-3 days, Class III, scenic
  • Bhote Koshi: 1-2 days, Class IV-V, extreme
  • Sun Koshi: 8-10 days, Class IV+, expedition
  • Kali Gandaki: 3 days, Class III-IV, remote
  • Marsyangdi: 2 days, Class IV+, challenging

Inclusions

  • All rafting equipment
  • Professional river guides
  • Safety kayakers
  • All meals on multi-day trips
  • Transportation to/from river
  • Camping equipment (multi-day)
  • First aid and safety equipment

What to Bring

  • Swimsuit/quick-dry clothes
  • River shoes/sandals
  • Sunscreen and hat
  • Waterproof camera
  • Personal medications
  • Change of clothes

Pricing

  • Trishuli (1 day): $45-65
  • Bhote Koshi (2 days): $120-180
  • Sun Koshi (10 days): $850-1,200

Prices vary by season and group size
