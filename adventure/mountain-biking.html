Mountain Biking

Conquer Himalayan trails on two wheels

Adventure Overview

Explore Nepal's stunning landscapes on thrilling mountain bike trails that wind through Himalayan valleys, traditional villages, and lush forests. From gentle countryside rides to challenging downhill tracks, we offer routes for every skill level.

Our guided tours include well-maintained bikes, experienced guides who know the best trails, and support vehicles for longer expeditions. Experience the freedom of cycling through some of the world's most breathtaking scenery.

Route Options

  • Kathmandu Valley: 1-3 days, cultural
  • Pokhara Trails: 2-4 days, scenic
  • Annapurna Circuit: 7-14 days, epic
  • Mustang Trails: 5-10 days, remote
  • Downhill Dhampus: 1 day, extreme

Inclusions

  • Quality mountain bike
  • Experienced biking guide
  • Helmet and safety gear
  • Support vehicle (multi-day)
  • Accommodation (multi-day)
  • All meals on tours
  • Basic repair kit

What to Bring

  • Cycling shorts
  • Padded gloves
  • Cycling shoes
  • Waterproof jacket
  • Personal water bottle
  • Energy snacks

Pricing

  • 3-Day Tour: $200-350
  • Week Expedition: $450-800
  • Custom Tour: Contact for quote
