Paragliding in Pokhara

Soar like a bird over the stunning Pokhara Valley

Adventure Overview

Experience the ultimate freedom of flight as you soar above the beautiful Pokhara Valley. With the Annapurna mountain range as your backdrop and Phewa Lake glistening below, paragliding in Pokhara offers one of the world's most scenic flying experiences.

All flights are conducted with certified pilots using modern, regularly inspected equipment. Tandem flights are perfect for beginners, while experienced pilots can opt for solo flights with advanced guidance.

Flight Options

  • Tandem Flight: 30-60 minutes, beginner-friendly
  • Cross-Country Flight: 1-2 hours, intermediate
  • Acrobatic Flight: 15-30 minutes, adrenaline rush
  • Photography Flight: 45-60 minutes, with GoPro
  • Sunset/Sunrise Flight: Special timing, magical views

Inclusions

  • Certified pilot (tandem flights)
  • Paragliding equipment
  • Safety harness and helmet
  • Transportation to launch site
  • Flight certificate
  • Basic insurance coverage
  • Photos/Video (optional)

What to Bring

  • Comfortable clothes
  • Closed-toe shoes
  • Sunglasses
  • Camera/GoPro (optional)
  • Windproof jacket
  • Water bottle

Pricing

  • Standard Tandem: $85-120
  • Cross-Country: $150-250
  • Acrobatic Flight: $120-180
  • Photography Package: $100-160 (with photos)

Prices vary by season and flight duration
