Helicopter Tour

See the Himalayas from a breathtaking perspective

Adventure Overview

Experience the ultimate Himalayan adventure with our exclusive helicopter tours. Fly over the world's highest mountains, landing at Everest Base Camp or circling the Annapurna massif for unparalleled views that few get to witness.

Our helicopter tours are operated by experienced pilots with extensive mountain flying experience. Modern, well-maintained helicopters ensure your safety and comfort as you enjoy one of the world's most spectacular aerial adventures.

Tour Options

  • Everest Base Camp: 4-5 hours, landing at Kalapatthar
  • Annapurna Circuit: 2-3 hours, panoramic mountain views
  • Langtang Valley: 2 hours, glaciers and peaks
  • Heli-Hiking Combo: Full day, flight + trekking
  • Private Charter: Custom itinerary

Inclusions

  • Helicopter flight
  • Experienced mountain pilot
  • All landing permits
  • Hotel pickup/dropoff
  • Oxygen support (Everest flights)
  • Breakfast (morning flights)
  • Flight certificate

What to Bring

  • Warm layers
  • Sunglasses
  • Camera with extra batteries
  • Passport/ID
  • Water bottle
  • Motion sickness pills (if needed)

Pricing

  • Everest Flight: $1,200-1,500 per person
  • Annapurna Flight: $800-1,000 per person
  • Langtang Flight: $600-800 per person
  • Private Charter: $3,000-5,000 per group

Group discounts available for 4+ people
