White Water Kayaking

Master the rapids in your own kayak

Adventure Overview

Take control of your own adventure with white water kayaking on Nepal's legendary rivers. From gentle beginner-friendly sections to challenging technical rapids, kayaking offers the ultimate personal river experience.

Our kayaking programs cater to all levels - from complete beginners to experienced paddlers. Learn essential skills from certified instructors or join guided trips on some of the world's best kayaking rivers.

Kayaking Options

  • Beginner Course: 2-3 days, basics
  • Intermediate Tour: 3-5 days, Trishuli
  • Advanced Expedition: 5-7 days, Sun Koshi
  • Day Kayaking: Full day, Pokhara lakes
  • Private Coaching: Custom, all levels

Inclusions

  • Kayak and paddle
  • Safety equipment
  • Certified instructor/guide
  • Spray skirt and helmet
  • Transport to river
  • Meals (multi-day)
  • Camping gear (expeditions)

What to Bring

  • Quick-dry clothing
  • River shoes
  • Sunscreen and lip balm
  • Waterproof camera
  • Personal medications
  • Swimsuit

Pricing

  • Beginner Course: $180-250
  • 3-Day Tour: $250-350
  • Week Expedition: $500-750
  • Private Day: $100-150

Prices vary by season and group size
