Wilderness Safari

Explore Nepal's wildlife-rich national parks

Adventure Overview

Venture into the heart of Nepal's tropical jungles for an unforgettable wildlife safari experience. Explore Chitwan or Bardia National Parks, home to rare species including Bengal tigers, one-horned rhinos, wild elephants, and over 500 bird species.

Our safari packages combine traditional elephant-back safaris, jungle walks, canoe trips, and cultural experiences with local Tharu communities. Expert naturalist guides enhance your chances of wildlife sightings and provide fascinating insights into the ecosystem.

Safari Options

  • Chitwan Classic: 3 days, elephant safari
  • Bardia Explorer: 4 days, tiger tracking
  • Luxury Safari: 3-5 days, premium lodges
  • Family Safari: 2-3 days, child-friendly
  • Photography Safari: 4-6 days, expert guiding

Inclusions

  • National park fees
  • Experienced naturalist guide
  • Accommodation in jungle lodges
  • All safari activities
  • All meals during safari
  • Transportation from Kathmandu
  • Cultural program

What to Bring

  • Neutral-colored clothing
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Binoculars and camera
  • Insect repellent
  • Sun hat and sunscreen
  • Light rain jacket

Pricing

  • 2-Day Safari: $120-180 per person
  • 3-Day Classic: $250-350 per person
  • 4-Day Explorer: $350-500 per person
  • Luxury Package: $500-800 per person

Prices based on double occupancy
